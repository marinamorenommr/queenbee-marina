Supporting local and organic honey


All of our honey is sourced in a sustainable and responsible way from our own apiaries in the beautiful Scottish Borders. We specialise in high-quality raw honey that is hand-packed with recycled materials. Our dedication to our bees is something deeply rooted in our company ethos.
