About Queen Bee


Who are we at Queen Bee?

We’re regularly referred to as a ‘hidden gem’ tucked away just in Melrose. We are an independent company built by friends who have a passion for beekeeping traditions. All of our honey is sourced in a sustainable and responsible way from our own apiaries in the beautiful Scottish Borders. We specialise in high-quality raw honey that is hand-packed with recycled materials. Our dedication to our bees is something deeply rooted in our company ethos.

Meet the Team!

We bought Queen Bee in the Summer of 2002 and fell in love with the name, the premises and its location in the picturesque Melrose city center. We are a group of friends passionate about delicious food, healthy bees, amazing honey and good vibes. We are always around Queen Bee, whether it is cooking, teaching about beekpeeing or just walking around. Please don't be shy and come and have a chat with us!

About our little bee workers

We are committed to our social responsibility and are working with local bee farmers to grow the bee farming industry in Scotland. With an urgent need for more bee farmers to meet the demand for Scottish honey, we are keen to support and develop new talent within the industry. Queen Bee utilise the best hives for the welfare of all our bees. Our hives are specially designed to nurture the bee population and specifically for keeping them safe during the cold Scottish winter months.
